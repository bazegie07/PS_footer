PetSuites Safety Precautions

The well-being of our guests, Pet Parents, and our team is, and always has been, our top priority, which is why we have implemented additional safety measures:

  • Contactless drop off/pick up/payment (curbside service)

  • Employees in proper PPE (masks, gloves)

  • Increased cleaning/sanitization

  • Wipe down of all dogs prior to going home

  • Social distancing of team members within resort
